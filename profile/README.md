# #WPLDN - Delivering WordPress-focused events in London

Free educational events focused on the open-source WordPress and PHP projects, running monthly in central London since 2008.

Every last Thursday of the month, 40-50 developers, designers, and business owners come together for talks, discussions, and community. All sessions are recorded and published under a Creative Commons licence.

## Get involved

- **Attend an event** — [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/london-wordpress/) / [Eventbrite](https://wpldn.eventbrite.co/)
- **Watch past talks** — [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@wpldn)
- **Apply to speak** — [wpldn.uk/speak](https://www.wpldn.uk/speak)

## Support #WPLDN

#WPLDN is free and always will be. If you'd like to help keep it that way, you can support us through [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/WPLDN). Every contribution goes toward venue hire, recording, and event essentials.
